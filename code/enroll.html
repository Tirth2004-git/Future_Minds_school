2024-2025 Enrollment

ENROLLMENT INFORMATION

The lottery procedure was held on (1/16/2024). We enrolled all new students first-come, first-served until we reached capacity.

For enrollment: Please visit the school website for detailed lottery procedures and dates.

  • You can visit our school office to receive and complete the enrollment form.
  • You can download an Enrollment Form from the right column of our website and complete and send it with the documents.
  • You can complete an Online Enrollment Form on our website, and we will contact you for signatures and documents.

Please bring the following documents to finalize your enrollment:

  • Proof of birth: Birth Certificate, Baptismal Certificate, or Passport.
  • Parent's photo ID: Guardianship documents if you are not the parent.
  • Address verification: Utility bill (DWP, Gas) for documentation purposes.
  • Physical Exam (for new-to-country or state students), vaccination record, and TB test.
  • The latest report card.
  • A recent copy of IEP (if applicable).
  • GATE program letter (if applicable).
